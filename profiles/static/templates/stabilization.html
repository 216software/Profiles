Stabilization

Digging further into housing dynamics and trends, Stabilization gives users a better sense of a neighborhood’s housing stability by providing data on occupancy, density, and transfers. Inversely, we can also learn more about areas of destabilized housing that may need to be flagged for attention due to foreclosure filings, sheriff’s sales, and/or other indicators of distress.
