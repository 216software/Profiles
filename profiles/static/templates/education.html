Source: Cleveland Metropolitan School District.
Note on KRAL Bands:

  • Band 1 - indicates children need immediate interventions in language & literacy skills, and possibly health (oral, vision, and/or medical)
  • Band 2 - indicates a need to watch children who may be scoring at the lower end and to assess for targeted instruction
  • Band 3 - indicates that children should do well with reading instruction and may need to be assessed for enrichment programs

Note: If data not available, it might be suppressed. Number and rate are not reported when the number of students who took the test is less than 10.
Note: The attendance rate is the average percentage of students attending school each day in the given year.
* Note: pre-school slots vary due to the opening and closing of schools, as well as changes in their quality ratings
